Stale Trading
Stale pricing reflects the old pricing and not the most recent adjusted price figures attached to the commodity.

Please be aware that any trade executed on the prices considered as ‘Stale Pricing’ will be voided and is of no reflection of the newly updated prices.
Misquotes Protocol
Agency execution model that automatically manages trader positions, utilising brokers and liquidity providers.
Occasions arising whereby prices are considered ‘skewed’, SGCFX reserves the right to perform a reversal on orders if a misquote occurs.
Investigation on these cases will be carried out and our traders will be immediately notified about a trade cancellation via email or telephone service
It is part of protocol to ensure reversals does not cause any unintended position.
SGCFX policy: Stale Quotes and Misquotes
In the event quoted prices are considered ‘stale’ or ‘skewed’, SGCFX reserves the right to cancel the respective trade orders.

Active monitoring and investigative practices will be carried out over the trading activity. If the trades are cancelled, notifications will be provided to the clients immediately.

Fair treatment for clients are part of our protocol and will be exercised consistently to ensure in the event of trade cancellations, that traders will not be at a disadvantage.
